About

She had a whirlwind romance with the world

& life itself loved her right back.

Aloha!

I’m Ashley!

I am a lover at my true nature.
You can feel my sweet presence & warm heart in that space I share with you. Quite literately, I was given the Thai name Waan (sweet) while traveling through Chiang Mai. My deepest wish is that it reaches through my words in raw form.

Im excited by the little things in life that…
bring tears to my face from laughing too hard,
sharing a smile with an absolute stranger while traveling on the road,
taking in a deep breath of fresh air as I sit on my yoga mat,
exploring little cafes in the alleyways of cute cities,
the empowering movement of solo travel,
playful shit talking & sarcastic dry humor,
soul to soul conversations that make you feel raw, vulnerable, & ridiculously human,
rolling around in the sand at the beach like a dog or child without a care in the world,
learning life from the mountains, oceans, desserts, & forests.
mysticism, spirit, soul, connection to our highest selves,
Leading leaders & empowering people to be the best version of themselves.

By trade I’m…

    A yoga teacher

  • My jam is teaching intimate classes that focus on a holistic approach to yoga.
    All things yin, meditation, restorative, & intuitive flows.


    A massage therapist

  • I love incorporating a variety of holistic modalities to specialize a session for each client. I specialize in deep tissue, Thai yoga massage, & prenatal.


    A health & lifestyle coach
  • Our team uses a system based in the highest quality + integrity that helps people transform their life from the inside out allowing for freedom in all areas of life.

...By trade I love all things Health & Wellness while having the freedom to live a life I love and travel my heart out while doing so.
