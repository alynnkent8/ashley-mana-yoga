Meet Ashley

Ashley grew up in Saratoga Springs, NY, where she began practicing yoga in 2006 under the guidance of Yoga Therapist Arletta Behan. While growing up, Ashley’s mother brought her to yoga classes and over the years she she fell in love with the practice and started to become curious how to help people heal, both physically and emotionally.

Ashley’s curiosity and passion lead her to complete a 200-Hour Multi-Style Yoga Teacher Training through Hawaii Yoga Institute in May of 2014 under the guidance of Professional Integrative Yoga Therapist, Adria Pickett. In pursuit of creating a holistic healing modality,, in February 2016 Ashley completed her training as a Licensed Massage Therapist through Hawaii Healing Arts College.

Ashley leads a mindful class that incorporates her knowledge of anatomy & physiology, pranayama, meditation, mantra, mudras, Thai Massage, and spirituality. Her intention as a teacher is to help students connect to their mind, body, and soul and to honor their space for personal growth. Ashley’s classes allow her students to look deeper and listen to the divine within through self-awareness and the embodiment of yoga.

Services

Private Yoga Sessions

Private sessions with Ashley provide individualized attention for students. Private sessions are a great way to work with special health concerns, injuries, or any other limitations you may experience. Private sessions are also a great option if you are working towards a goal and need some help getting there. Whether you are new to yoga or want to deepen your practice, anyone and everyone can benefit from private yoga sessions.

Massage Therapy

Ashley uses a combination of healing modalities for her clients holistic wellbeing. She specializes in deep tissue, clinical massage therapy, Thai yoga massage, prenatal, and relaxation.

Health and Lifestyle Coaching

life is too short to live anything but an EXTRAORDINARY life. Through her commitment to a healthy lifestyle, hard work, and dedication, Ashley leads leaders to take ownership of their future lives with proven systems by being of service to others and helping people with their health and wellness.

Group Yoga Classes

Ashley teaches various group classes on the island of Oahu. Group classes are the most affordable option for individuals seeking to create a steady practice and explore their yoga journey. Group classes allow individuals to be involved in a yoga community as the students support each other on their own journeys through yoga. Group classes are usually 90 minutes long.

Thai Massage Workshops

Are you ready to deepen your practice? Thai Yoga Massage Workshops are specialized classes that focus on balancing energy lines and thai yoga stretching. Workshops provide a in-depth experience, knowledge, and understanding of yoga which is not always emphasized in group classes. Yoga workshops are usually two hours long.

Corporate Yoga

Bringing yoga into your workplace strategically positions you as an employer to support your employees's well-being and to meet your deadlines. Offering yoga in the workplace enhances clarity and concentration while also cultivating a strong teamwork quality
